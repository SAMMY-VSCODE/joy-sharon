Groom's Story

It all started in the month of October 2020
when this new girl moved in to a lodge I was also
staying. It was time for our evening prayers at the lodge and as I led prayers that night, this “new girl” started singing and the whole atmosphere became charged.

Inwardly, I whispered to myself, "I love this grace,"
after the prayers ended, we all introduced
ourselves to the New girl.

Several weeks had passed since our first encounter when,
I spotted her wearing a geology T-shirt.
Excited by the shared interest since I also studied Geology in Uni,
I seized the opportunity to strike up a
conversation with her on the subject.
Little did I know that this casual encounter
would become the catalyst for something truly special.

We became really close friends and
as our friendship blossomed, I eventually came
to the decision that we should be best friends forever.

Bride's Story

In October 2020, I finally received the long-awaited approval
from my parents to relocate to Akure,
as the Lord had already instructed me about my impending move in the previous year.
During that year, my friend was planning her wedding,
so she offered me her place to stay pending when
I would find a new place since her rent had not yet expired.

The very day I got to Akure, at the lodge where my friend stayed,
another mutual friend of ours invited
me for a prayer meeting that regularly held at the lodge.
Reluctantly, I joined although I was very tired from the journey.

That evening, there was this young man who led the prayer session
with so much passion and unwavering authority.
The moment I saw this young man, something leaped in my heart and
there was a knowing in my spirit that
this is my husband but I wondered how this shall be since there was
someone else I was already planning to say “Yes” to.

At first the 'young man' was very unfriendly (or so I assumed)
of all the others who stayed at the lodge
so I quickly discarded every prompting I had in my spirit
towards him until one day — the day he engaged
me in a conversation upon seeing me in a “geology” inscribed t-shirt…

Till date, we have never gone a day without talking to each other. The rest they say is history...s
